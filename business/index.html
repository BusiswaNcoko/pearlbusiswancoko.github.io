About Us

LIziwe Academy is an after school programme that is aimed to help everyone in South Africa to have access to High quality of education for free. Liziwe Academy was founded in 2010 with the aim to help South Africans with limited resources to have access to education.The academy specialises with STEM subjects. We help students from Primary School to First year level of Tertiary education. We train students to become the best versions of themselves and to love learning.
